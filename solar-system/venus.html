Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is often called Earth's "sister planet" due to their similar size and composition. However, Venus has a thick, toxic atmosphere that traps heat, making it the hottest planet.

Fun Fact: Venus has a day that is longer than its year, as it takes about 243 Earth days to rotate once on its axis, but only 225 days to orbit the Sun.
