jupiter

Mass: 1.898 × 1027 kg

Rotation Speed: 45,300 km/h (at the equator)

Orbital Period: 12 years


Jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system and is primarily composed of hydrogen and helium. It has a strong magnetic field and more than 75 moons.

Fun Fact: Jupiter’s Great Red Spot is a massive storm that has been raging for at least 400 years, and it’s larger than Earth itself.
