neptune

Mass: 1.024 × 1026 kg

Rotation Speed: 9,720 km/h (at the equator)

Orbital Period: 165 years


Neptune is the eighth and farthest planet from the Sun. It’s a blue gas giant known for its intense winds, which are the fastest in the solar system.

Fun Fact: Neptune has a large storm similar to Jupiter’s Great Red Spot, called the Great Dark Spot, which was first observed in 1989 by the Voyager 2 spacecraft.
